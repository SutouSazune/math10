About Math Speed

Math Speed is an innovative tool designed to help users improve their mathematical skills through speed and accuracy. Whether you're a student, teacher, or math enthusiast, Math Speed offers a fun and engaging way to practice math.

Features

- Interactive math challenges to test your speed and accuracy.

- Progress tracking to monitor your improvement over time.

- Customizable difficulty levels to suit all skill levels.

How to Use

1. Select a difficulty level that matches your skill.

2. Start solving math problems as quickly as you can.

3. Track your progress and aim to improve your score!
